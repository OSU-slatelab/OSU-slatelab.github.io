Speech and Language Technologies Lab

The OSU Speech and Language Technologies Lab has conducted research in a variety of applied machine learning settings, particularly for natural language processing, automatic speech recognition and spoken dialog systems. We are a proud part of the Artificial Intelligence group in Computer Science & Engineering as well as the Computational Linguistics and Language Technology interdiscipinary group.
